Our Story

Cooking with Passion, Serving with Heart

Ember & Oak was born from a simple belief: that great food should be honest, seasonal, and full of character. Our chefs source ingredients from local farmers every morning and craft each dish fresh.

From intimate dinners to large celebrations, we create the setting that lets your moments shine. Every detail — from the wine list to the candlelight — is chosen with care.
